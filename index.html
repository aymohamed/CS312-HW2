My name is Abdi and I am a native of the Horn of Africa.
The Horn of Africa is the peninsula in NorthEast Africa and
it consists of Ethiopia, Kenya , Djibouti, Somalia and Eritrea.
The Horn of Africa is popular for its spicy cuisines, diverse cultures
and its geopolitical strategy.The Gulf of Aden located in the Horn
is one of the busiest waterways in the world with close to 21,000 ships
crossing annually.
